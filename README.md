# Fantasy_MM
A functional web game for playing fantasy march madness. Pick your players from your favorite teams and watch them score you points as the tournament rages on! 

League rules:

2-10 teams per league
10 NCAA Basketball players per team

Draft Rules:

Draft order is randomized

Snake draft, one player to each team per round, once a player is picked they are no longer available.

Each team selects 7 players from NCAA teams seeded 1-6, 2 players on NCAA teams seeded 7-10 and 1 player from an NCAA team seeded 11 - 16.

Scoring Rules:

After each game each players points get added to their teams total. 
If a NCAA team gets eliminated, that player gets points from their elimination game but cannot score any more points the rest of the tournament.

For each NCAA players final score, add up the players total points from all their played games.
For each teams total score, add up all players points on that team.
